Something about my Life

Born on 29th April 1986 in Belgrade, Serbia in small suburbia Petlovo Brdo which has lots of new buildings. Elementary School and High School finished until 2005 in the same neighborhood . Along with education I was playing football in local footbal clubs, and after 2005 I have continued playing for several years in Serbia and abroad. In 2009 I have entered college for Business Entrepreneurship with full sports schoolarship as futsal player. Finished bachelor degree in 2012 And after that continued in same college for Master degree in Economics. In December 2013 I started working in Telenor Serbia as Customer Care and Sales representative, next position was as Advanced Customer Technical Support Representative in Subotica town in north of Serbia on border with Hungary. Currently I am working in IT sector of Telenor Common Operation as Business Application Support Analyst for Telenor Denmark form February 2017 and continuesly improving my skills as Developer.
